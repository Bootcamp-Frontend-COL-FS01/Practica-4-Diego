Tic Tac Toe :)

Welcome guests! Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game that is typically played on a 3x3 grid. The game is also known as "noughts and crosses" or "Xs and Os".

The game is played by two players who take turns marking spaces on the grid with their respective symbols. Player 1 typically uses "X" as their symbol while player 2 uses "O". The objective of the game is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Player (O)

A good strategy for player 1 in Tic Tac Toe is to start by placing their first "O" mark in the center square of the grid. This move provides the greatest number of options for creating a winning pattern, as it allows for the possibility of creating a row of three symbols in any direction.

Player (X)

A good strategy for player 2 in Tic Tac Toe is to start by placing their first "X" mark in one of the corner squares of the grid. This move creates more potential winning patterns for player 2, as it allows for the possibility of creating two diagonal patterns and one horizontal or vertical pattern.
